Spike extraction from dopamine raster image

Method:
- Manually cropped the three raster panels.
- Detected spike-like blobs with Laplacian-of-Gaussian blob detection.
- Explicitly masked vertical border lines and the central event line before blob detection.
- Rendered a reconstruction with thickened lines and filled dot disks to maximize dark-pixel coverage.
- Converted x-position to seconds using panel-specific anchors read from the figure.

Caveats:
- This is approximate digitization from a low-resolution image, not ground truth.
- Spikes that sit exactly on the vertical event line can be undercounted.
- y-coordinates are preserved as pixel positions; no attempt was made to assign discrete trial numbers.
